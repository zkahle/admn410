Our History

It all started when my father caught a fish in front of me at the age of 4. The blood. The mess. The hook. It was all too hard to watch. I asked my father why we have to kill the fish when we have food at home. He replied, "Because it's the only way I know how to catch a fish." From that day forward, I was determined to find a new way to capture a fish without injuring them. I tried numerous methods on fake fish to see what would work. Eventually, the glass dome fishing rod came to life, and the rest is history!

Why Use Us?

The Glass Dome Fishing Rod functions as a normal rod does, but holds a small glass aquarium-like contraption on the bottom. There is a sliding door with the bait inside which draws the fish inside. Once the fish is captured, the door will slide shut and a leak proof seal will be created. A child can view the fish close up without injuring the wildlife. This is a sustainable way for our future generations to enjoy the beaty that lies beneath the sea. By purchasing a glass dome rod, you can make a difference in the lives of our children.

Our Product

The Dome Fishing Rod

The sealed trapped door is a game-changer in the fishing world.


Step 1

The glass dome fills with water and invites the fish in with bait located near the entrance.

Step 2

The door creates a vacuum seal and can be lifted out of the water without harming the fish. This is a more sustainable way of viewing the fish up close.


Step 3

The fishing rod now acts as an aquarium for the fish and allows many children to learn about the life under the sea without harming our wildlife. Creating a brighter future by limiting the number of fish casualties caused by recreation fishing.
